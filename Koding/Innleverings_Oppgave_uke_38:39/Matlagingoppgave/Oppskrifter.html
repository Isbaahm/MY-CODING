Todays recipe:

Baked salmon with chilli potatoes

Other recepies:

This quick and easy recipe for oven baked salmon is ready in about 15 minutes. Enjoy tender, flaky salmon with just the right seasoning, cooked to perfection in your oven. No extra steps, no messy cleanup, just delicious fresh salmon made with ease. Our salmon filets will be ready to serve right away. One of my favorite ways to serve is with a vegetable medley. You can actually place the veggies right on the sheet pan with the salmon. They will be done at the same time the salmon is done. Or, the veggies can be roasted to perfection in an air fryer in about 10 minutes!
